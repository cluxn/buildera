- motion imports must be from "motion/react" — NEVER "framer-motion"
- "use client" ONLY on: forms, interactive widgets, animated leaf components
- TipTap: `immediatelyRender: false` is required on `useEditor()` to avoid Next.js SSR hydration mismatches
- TipTap v3: `BubbleMenu` is imported from `@tiptap/react/menus`, NOT `@tiptap/extension-bubble-menu` directly (that package is just a peer dep)
